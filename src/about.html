Concert agencies

Nowadays it is quite difficult to be a professional musician. Bands often faces with difficulties of organisation, because, first of all, it's not easy at all, and also they have a lot of theirs own deals to do. That's why concert agencies exists. Concert agencies are institution, which help musicians with organisation tasks and let them care about art only.

Cactus

Cactus is one of the most famous concert agencies in Europe. Since it was founded in 2007 Cactus worked together with such musicians as Pasosh, Paws, Sonic Youth, Paramore, The Kooks and others. Our clients become our friends and they appreciate quailty of our work. We have powerful and creative team, which can deal with any difficulties on the way. We organise single concerts, concert tours and festivals. We are open to work with both famous and underground musicians.
